## Main observation

After treated-control correction, transcriptome-wide candidate C-to-U editing sites can be divided into low, moderate, and strong editing groups. Low-to-moderate candidate sites are expected to be more common than high-efficiency candidate sites.

The candidate table provides positive samples for downstream sequence-based modeling, while clean background C sites from the all-C table provide negative samples.
